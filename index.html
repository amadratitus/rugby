About Us

The Kyambogo University Rugby Club is a thriving sports club dedicated to promoting the sport of rugby among students. We provide a platform for students to learn and play rugby in a competitive yet friendly environment. Our club focuses on developing skills, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

Join us today and experience the thrill of rugby!
