About Us

At Kyambogo University Rugby Club, we pride ourselves on fostering a culture of teamwork, discipline, and personal growth. Our dedicated coaches and experienced players are dedicated to nurturing talent and promoting the values of rugby both on and off the field.
We provide a platform for individuals with a passion for rugby to come together, hone their skills, and compete at various levels of the sport.
As a member of the club, you will have access to top-notch training facilities and resources that will enable you to develop your rugby abilities to their fullest potential. Whether you are a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced player seeking to take your game to the next level, our club offers a supportive and inclusive environment for players of all skill levels.

Join us today and experience the thrill of rugby!
